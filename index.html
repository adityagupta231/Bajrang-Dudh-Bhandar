Our Story

Crafted with Love, Since 2023

Bajrang doodh bhandar opened in 2023 with a simple promise — to deliver the purest, freshest dairy products from our farm to your family table.

Today, we partner with over 200 local farms, but our promise remains unchanged. Every bottle of milk, every scoop of ice cream, and every spoonful of dahi is made with the same care we'd give our own family.

  • ✓ 200+ partner farms across the region
  • ✓ Zero preservatives, zero artificial colors
  • ✓ Sustainable & eco-friendly packaging
